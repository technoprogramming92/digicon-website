ABOUT US

A company involved in
Plastic machinery manufacturing.

Founded in 2012, Digicon Machinery stands at the forefront of manufacturing High-Speed Heater Cooler Mixers and Pulverizers, catering mainly to the PVC Pipe, Profile, Rotational Moulding, and PVC Fitting Industries.

With its deep-rooted expertise in the plastic industry, Digicon has established itself as a provider of top-notch machinery, underscored by a team of professional engineers dedicated to innovation and quality.

Serving a worldwide clientele - including key markets in Europe, Asia, and the Americas, Digicon Machinery is focused on customer satisfaction and is dedicated to becoming a global leader in mixing and pulverizer technology.

Our commitment to continually adapting to the evolving needs of the industry, coupled with their pursuit of excellence, positions them as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide.
